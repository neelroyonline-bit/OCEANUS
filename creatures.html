ü§î WHAT IF Scenarios

What if deep-sea creatures came to the surface?

Most would die instantly! They're adapted to crushing pressures (up to 1,000 ATM). At the surface, their bodies would expand, their gas-filled swim bladders would rupture, and the temperature change would be lethal. Some species literally "melt" when brought up too quickly.

What if we could see all bioluminescent creatures at once?

The deep ocean would light up like a galaxy! Scientists estimate that 76% of ocean animals produce light. The deep sea contains more bioluminescence than all the stars visible in the night sky. It would be the most spectacular light show on Earth.

What if the giant squid's eyes were on land animals?

A human with proportional eyes would have eyes 30cm (1 foot) in diameter! Giant squid eyes evolved to detect the faint bioluminescence of approaching sperm whales from 120 meters away in total darkness.
