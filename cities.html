ü§î WHAT IF Scenarios

What if Atlantis was real and we found it?

It would rewrite human history! Plato described Atlantis as more advanced than ancient Greece ‚Äî with hot and cold running water, advanced architecture, and powerful technology. Finding it would prove advanced civilizations existed 11,000+ years ago, predating our earliest known cities by millennia.

What if sea levels continue rising?

Within 100 years, major coastal cities could join the list of sunken cities! Miami, New York, Shanghai, Mumbai, and London could face the same fate as Dwarka and Port Royal. By 2100, 200+ million people may be displaced by rising seas.

What if we could drain the Mediterranean for a day?

We would find thousands of sunken cities, ships, and artifacts! The Mediterranean has been a hub of civilization for 10,000+ years. Ancient ports, trading vessels, and entire civilizations lie preserved in its depths. It would be the greatest archaeological discovery in history.
